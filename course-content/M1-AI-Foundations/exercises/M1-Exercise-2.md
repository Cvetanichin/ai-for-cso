# Exercise 2: Evaluating AI Tools for CSO Work

**Accompanies Lesson 3 — AI Risk Literacy for Non-Profits**
**Time required:** 15 minutes

## Instructions

1. Pick one AI tool you currently use, or are considering, at your organisation.
2. Run it through the AI Risk Assessment Checklist (in your Starter Kit, file: `AI-Risk-Assessment-Checklist.pdf`).
3. Answer every question honestly — including the ones that are inconvenient.
4. Decide: adopt as-is, adopt with safeguards, or do not adopt.
5. Write a two-sentence justification for your decision.

## Reflection Prompt

If you answered "no" to any of the first three data-privacy questions, what would it take to make that tool safe to use — and is that realistic for your organisation in the next three months?

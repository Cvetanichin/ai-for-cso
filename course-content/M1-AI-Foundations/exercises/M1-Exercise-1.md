# Exercise 1: Crafting Your First NGO-Specific Prompt

**Accompanies Lesson 2 — Prompt Design for NGO Workflows**
**Time required:** 10 minutes

## Instructions

1. Pick one real, recurring task from your own work — a donor email, a Q&A response, a social post, a report summary.
2. Write a **weak** version of the prompt (one sentence, no role/context/constraints).
3. Rewrite it using the four-step formula:
   - **Role** — who is the AI acting as?
   - **Task** — what exactly should it produce?
   - **Context** — what background does it need?
   - **Constraints** — length, tone, format?
4. Run both versions through Claude (or the Prompt Optimizer web app) and compare the outputs.
5. Note what changed.

## Worked Example

**Weak prompt:** "Write a donor update."

**Strong prompt:** "You are a programme officer for a youth employment NGO in Kosovo. Draft a 150-word donor update for our quarterly EU report. Focus on the 40 young people placed in internships this quarter. Tone: professional, data-led."
